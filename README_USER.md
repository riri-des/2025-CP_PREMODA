# PREMODA - Your Virtual Fashion Assistant 🛍️

## Welcome to the Future of Online Shopping!

PREMODA is a revolutionary mobile application that transforms your online shopping experience by letting you virtually try on clothes before you buy them. No more guessing how that dress will look on you - see it for yourself with our cutting-edge computer vision technology!

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎯 Virtual Try-On
- **See Before You Buy**: Take a photo or use your camera to see how clothes look on your body
- **Real-Time Preview**: Get instant results with our advanced AI technology
- **Multiple Items**: Try on multiple clothing items at once

### 🛒 Smart Shopping
- **Easy Navigation**: Browse through Women's, Men's, and Kids' collections
- **Smart Search**: Find exactly what you're looking for with our intelligent search
- **Category Filters**: Shop by type - dresses, shirts, polos, and more

### 📱 User-Friendly Interface
- **Beautiful Design**: Elegant cream and beige theme with modern aesthetics
- **Smooth Experience**: Intuitive navigation with responsive design
- **Quick Access**: Bottom navigation for easy access to all features

## 📲 How It Works

1. **Browse**: Explore our extensive catalog of fashion items
2. **Select**: Add items to your cart that you want to try
3. **Try On**: Use your camera or upload a photo
4. **See Results**: View yourself wearing the selected items
5. **Shop**: Make confident purchasing decisions

## 🔒 Privacy & Security
- Your photos are processed securely
- Images are deleted after processing
- No personal data is stored without permission

## 💡 Tips for Best Results
- Use good lighting when taking photos
- Stand against a plain background
- Wear fitted clothing for accurate results
- Keep your full body in frame

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. Download PREMODA from your app store
2. Allow camera permissions
3. Start browsing and trying on clothes!
